Mes Amis et Moi

What is this site?

From February 15th to February 24th, I got to travel to France and Spain. Anika, Aimee, Emily,
Josh, Kyra, Sarah, and I spent our winter break traveling with students at another high school in the
district.I felt really lucky I got to go on this trip (special thank you to my mom), especially since going
with my friends made it an even more incredible and fun experience. I created this site so I could write
about the memories we made each day, what we saw and learned, and so I could post these pictures.
